About AyosPH

Empowering communities through transparent issue reporting

Building Better Barangays Together

AyosPH is a government technology platform designed to strengthen the relationship between residents and barangay officials. By making issue reporting transparent and trackable, we help communities solve problems faster.

Our mission is to empower barangay residents with the tools they need to report issues effectively, and to help barangay officials manage and resolve problems with full transparency and proof-based completion.
